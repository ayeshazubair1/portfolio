About Me

With a background in Economics, I developed an interest in Data Analytics within the Retail domain, focusing on how operational systems behave and how well-structured analytical questions reveal patterns that influence real business outcomes. My work strongly emphasizes data structure, validation, and clarity—ensuring analysis is built on reliable foundations, not assumptions. My projects follow an end-to-end analytical process, from raw data preparation to insight generation, with a focus on both data integrity and analytical reasoning. Retail remains my primary area of interest due to the complexity of its everyday operations — from customer behavior to inventory flow and revenue performance. Analyzing these systems provides a practical way to understand how businesses function at scale.
My current work continues to explore retail-focused analyses while strengthening my technical and analytical capabilities.
